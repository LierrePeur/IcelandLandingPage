MYSTERIOUS BEACH WALK

The Reynisfjara Black Sand Beach is a beautiful and eminent attraction
in Iceland. The black sand of the beach strikes a flawless contrast with
the spume of the white-crowned waves and the ocean blue water. The
National Geographic and Conde Nast Traveller have ranked this beach to
be one of the most beautiful beaches on the planet. As a result that,
this beach has also made it to the top attractions on the bucket list of
travelers.
